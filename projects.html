Minus 1

In this project called Minus 1, a third-person shooter game, my work focused on level design, enemy placement, and environment design. The goal was to create fast-paced, intense combat sequences where players feel like an unstoppable, highly-skilled action hero, smoothly navigating through dynamic environments while taking on waves of enemies with precision and style. The fluidity of the combat and strategic enemy positioning were designed to enhance the player's sense of power and mastery over each level.
